About

A decade at the edge of two worlds.

58th St. Digital is the practice of Daniel Kennedy — a finance executive and strategist with a decade of experience operating at the convergence of traditional and digital finance.

Daniel has served in director-level roles at public companies, led investor relations, capital markets, and strategic communications mandates, and advised organizations navigating the frontier of blockchain and digital assets.

His work spans earnings strategy, RegFD compliance, capital allocation, pre-IPO advisory, and investor introductions — with a fluency in both the language of institutional finance and the culture of Web3.
